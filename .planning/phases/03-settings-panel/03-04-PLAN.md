**SETT-05 — Retake Test button:**
9. Confirm: a "Retake Test" button is visible below the strength selector

**SETT-06 — Panel collapses:**
10. Click the × button in the panel header — confirm: panel slides out and window shrinks back to ~286px
11. Click the hamburger to open panel again, then click the hamburger again — confirm: same collapse behavior

12. Click Start to begin the timer, then open and close the settings panel — confirm: countdown continues uninterrupted
